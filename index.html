Clean TIX bookmarklet

The TIX Matrix can take up quite some space, whichs makes it difficult to use.

This bookmarklet cleans it up, so that you can focus on filling in those hours!

  1. Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: Clean TIX.
  2. Navigate to the TIX Matrix.
  3. Press the bookmarket.
  4. That's it!
